## 🎯 Trends Summary

**June 2026 is dominated by:**

1. **Agent-First Everything** — AI agents moving from hype to production. 40% of enterprise apps will feature task-specific agents by end of 2026.
2. **Private/Local AI** — Privacy-first, offline-capable AI running on personal devices. Ollama + Open WebUI is the standard stack.
3. **Multi-Agent Orchestration** — Not one AI doing everything, but teams of specialized agents collaborating (CrewAI, AutoGen, MetaGPT).
4. **Visual AI Builders** — Langflow, Dify, Flowise are making AI accessible without deep coding knowledge.
5. **Developer Tooling Renaissance** — Rust-based tools (Biome, Ruff, Zed) offer 10-100x speedups.
6. **Memory & Context** — Persistent AI memory (Mem0) solving the stateless interaction problem.
7. **Crypto Maturation** — Shift from speculation to infrastructure: Foundry, viem, Uniswap V4 hooks.
